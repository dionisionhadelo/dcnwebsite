Last update in 17/Aug/2021

Who is?

Dionisio Nhadelo is a Mozambican citizen born in Maputo province on June 21st of 1994. He is a former student from Physics Department, Faculty of Science at Eduardo Mondlane University where he did get a degree in Atmospheric Science. He is also a former DARA Project Student for the 2018-2019 Basic Training Program.

He is currently engaged in learning new technologies related to Data Science. He got interested in Data Science during his participation in the DARA Project, where there was a lot of scientific talks and hands on in radio astronomy. This project was held in Maputo at Eduardo Mondlane University and HarTRAO.


Dionisio and Ramiro Saide were tutors of the first event of DARA BIG DATA HACKATHON in Mozambique, they did organize and run it with a huge support and collaboration of PhD Cláudio Moisés Paulo from Eduardo Mondlane University, PhD Nikhita Madhanpall from SAAO and Linzi Stirrup from University of Manchester.



After the greater success of the hackathon, Dionisio was invited to work as tutor as well. Not alone, he worked as tutor for High Performance Computing (HPC), introduction to Linux command line, bash scripting and for an introduction in Python programming for the Mozambican cohort 3 DARA students, as well to assist the Data Redaction and Imaging unit tutored by James Chibueze, this opportunity was trusted by PhD Cláudio Moisés Paulo coordinator of DARA Project in Mozambique.
